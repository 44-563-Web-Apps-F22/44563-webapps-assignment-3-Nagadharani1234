Sita Ramam

Ram (Dulquer Salmaan) is a Lieutenant officer in the Indian Army. Sita (Mrunal Thakur) writes a letter to the orphaned Ram mentioning her as his wife. So Ram travels in search of Sita who loves him. Events in Ram's life after meeting Sita lead to their separation.Did Ram and Sita meet? Did Sita accept Ram's love? What incident hindered their love? Why Afreen (Rashmika Mandanna) from Pakistan tries to give Sita a letter written by Ram? What is Ram's relation to Afreen? Finally, Sita Ramam is the answer to the question of what is the end of Ram and Sita's love story.
